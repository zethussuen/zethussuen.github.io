Orion – Programming Calendar

A departure from linear television means a departure from traditional programming. No longer does distribution rely on filling time slots, rather the key to programming in a digital age is efficiency. Giving each title the best shot it has to succeed in an ever-expanding catalog is not a trivial problem to solve. We partnered with data science teams within Netflix to help us look across hundreds of thousands of internal and external titles. Coupled with date resctrictions (award qualifications, production schedules, etc.) and viewership prediction, Netflix is able to confidently space out and promote its original content to the fullest.
