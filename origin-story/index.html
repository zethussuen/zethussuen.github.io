Origin Story – Production Hub

By 2015, Netflix's studios began to outgrow Google Docs and other CMS tools to manage their productions. With the production operations team in Beverly Hills, we began drafting the idea of an ecosystem of applications and microservices that would one day track all of Netflix's production details. Origin Story became a one-stop shop for budgets, cast, crew, locations, schedules, and much more. As our studio evolved, the app became more fragmented and harder to maintain across different content verticals. Going into the future, Origin Story's individual modules will be broken into stand-alone microservices that will allow for its data to be better expressed as the business changes its goals.
